Q: What are the aromatic R groups?
A: Phenylalanine, Tyrosine, Tryptophan

Q: What are the polar, uncharged R groups?
A: Serine, Threonine, Cysteine, Asparagine, Glutamine

Q: What are the positively charged R groups?
A: Lysine, Arginine, Histidine

Q: What are the negatively charged R groups?
A: Aspartate, Glutamate
